# FarmKit

FarmKit is a Malawi-focused digital agribusiness platform designed to improve access to agricultural information, market opportunities, and practical decision-making tools.

It is being built to support farmers, buyers, sellers, cooperatives, agribusinesses, NGOs, and other agricultural stakeholders through one connected system.

## Overview

Agriculture depends heavily on access to timely information, reliable markets, and trusted connections. In many cases, these are scattered, inconsistent, or difficult to access efficiently.

FarmKit aims to solve that by creating a platform where users can access agricultural knowledge, track prices, identify suppliers, understand market trends, and connect with buyers and sellers more effectively.

## Problem

Many agricultural participants face challenges such as:

- limited access to reliable farming information
- difficulty finding current market prices
- weak links between buyers and sellers
- poor visibility of where to buy inputs and tools
- lack of simple digital tools for planning and decision-making
- fragmented communication between institutions and end users

## Solution

FarmKit brings multiple agricultural needs into one system by combining information, market visibility, and user interaction in a practical and scalable platform.

The goal is to make agriculture more informed, connected, and efficient.

## Core Features

FarmKit is planned to include the following key functions:

### 1. Agricultural Information
Users can access useful agricultural information to support better decisions in both crop and livestock production.

### 2. Price Tracking
The platform tracks changes in prices for agricultural goods and inputs, helping users understand inflation, pricing patterns, and market movements.

### 3. Where to Buy Goods
FarmKit helps users identify where they can buy important agricultural goods such as seeds, fertilizer, pesticides, feed, and tools.

### 4. Market Performance Summaries
The platform provides weekly and monthly summaries to show how markets are performing and where stronger opportunities may exist.

### 5. Crop Farming Guides
Users can access guidance on crop production, including planning, budgeting, input use, and practical production support.

### 6. Livestock Farming Guides
FarmKit also supports livestock producers with information on planning, feed sources, markets, and production tools.

### 7. Weather Updates
Weather information is included to help users plan agricultural activities more effectively.

### 8. Agricultural Calculators
The platform is expected to include calculators that help users with budgeting, planning, and farm-related estimates.

### 9. Buyer and Seller Interaction
Sellers can list products and services, while buyers can create profiles and post what they are looking for.

### 10. Institutional Communication
NGOs, cooperatives, and agricultural institutions can use the platform to share relevant information with target users.

## Target Users

FarmKit is intended for:

- farmers
- buyers
- sellers
- agro-dealers
- cooperatives
- estates
- NGOs
- agricultural service providers
- institutions and support organisations

## Vision

FarmKit is being built as more than just an app or website. The long-term vision is to create practical digital agricultural infrastructure that can improve how information, trade, and support systems work in Malawi.

## Product Direction

The platform is being shaped around several core principles:

- practical value over unnecessary complexity
- accessibility for everyday users
- trust and usefulness before aggressive expansion
- systems that can scale over time
- strong relevance to Malawi’s agricultural environment

## Business Model Direction

FarmKit is expected to operate with a mixed-access approach.

Some features may remain free for broad accessibility, while certain commercial users such as sellers may pay through monthly subscriptions or commissions on sales. The exact structure is still being refined as the platform develops.

## Current Status

FarmKit is currently under development.

The project is in its planning and structuring stage, with focus on defining the platform model, refining features, and building a strong long-term foundation.

## Long-Term Goal

The long-term goal is to build a trusted agricultural ecosystem that improves:

- access to information
- market coordination
- visibility of agricultural opportunities
- trade connections
- decision-making across the agricultural value chain

## Conclusion

FarmKit is a long-term agribusiness platform project focused on solving real agricultural access and coordination problems in Malawi through practical digital systems.
